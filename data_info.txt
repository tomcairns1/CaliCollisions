The info from the party table:

case_id = the unique identifier of the collision report

party_age = age of party at time of collision (int)
            998 - unknown

party_sobriety = sobriety of party in collision
                A - Had not been drinking
                B - Had been drinking, under influence
                C - Had been drinking, not under influence
                D - Had been drinking, impairment unknown
                G - Impairment unknown
                H - Not applicable
